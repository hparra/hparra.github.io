search -- The Problem of Search
===============================

## Searching Strings

Finding a instances of a substring within a string is a fundamental problem.
There are a few methods to do so:
- Brute Force -- O(m*n)
- Rabin-Karp
- Knuth-Morris-Pratt
- Boyer-Moore

## Searching Trees and Graphs

- Depth-First Search
- Breadth-First Search
- Belmman-Ford
- Floyd-Warshall
- Dijkstra 
- A*
